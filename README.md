# Learning Management System (LMS) - Minimum Viable Product

## Project Overview
Welcome to our new Learning Management System (LMS)! Our platform is designed to provide a seamless educational experience for both educators and students, offering essential learning management tools with an added layer of collaboration for teachers. Unlike traditional systems, our LMS integrates a feedback-driven environment where educators can submit and receive insights on their curriculum, lesson plans, and teaching methodologies. Our AI-powered system further enhances lesson plan improvements by providing data-driven recommendations.

## Usage Guide
1. **Sign Up/Login** - Teachers and students create an account to access the platform.
2. **Create & Manage Courses** - Educators can upload resources, assignments, and manage student progress.
3. **Submit & Receive Feedback** - Teachers can share lesson plans and receive constructive feedback.
4. **AI Assistance** - Educators receive AI-driven insights and recommendations to refine their teaching materials.
5. **Engage with Students** - Utilize quizzes, discussions, and grading tools to enhance the learning experience.
